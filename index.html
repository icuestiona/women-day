International Women's Day

International Women's Day is a global day celebrating the social, economic, cultural, and political achievements of women. The day also marks a call to action for accelerating gender parity.
Each year, International Women's Day is celebrated on March 8th. The first International Women's Day was celebrated in 1911, and the day has been observed in various ways around the world ever since.
Join us as we recognize the achievements and struggles of women throughout history and around the globe.

International Women's Day March

March 8, 2025

This global event held annually on March 8th also celebrates women's social, economic, cultural, and political achievements. The march is also a call for gender equality and raises awareness about issues such as gender-based violence, reproductive rights, and equal pay. Participants march in solidarity with women worldwide and demand that their voices be heard. Join us for a peaceful march to raise awareness for women's rights and gender equality.
